My Services

I offer individual counselling in English to adolescents and young adults who may be experiencing difficulty related to school, university, work, relocation, family, relationships or any other personal concerns.

In a warm, gentle manner that is respectful of the uniqueness of the individual, I work together with the individual to understand and address the challenges being faced. Listening attentively and objectively, I use my knowledge and skills to explore and clarify underlying issues that might contribute to distress. Through greater insight and encouragement, the individual is in a position to improve the situation and face the tasks of life with a sense of confidence. Working in full cooperation with the individual, I endeavor to support and guide the individual in this venture of self-discovery and social betterment.
