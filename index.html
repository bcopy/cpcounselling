WELCOME

Both adolescence and young adulthood are periods of transition and great transformation in one’s life. This coming of age may be accompanied by changes that are often stressful and confusing. Many emerging adults struggle with decisions about the future, low confidence, identity confusion, and emotional hardships during this time.
 
Counselling provides you with a confidential space in which you can talk openly about your concerns. You can expect to be heard and encouraged as we work together to explore your difficulties with the aim of understanding and addressing them. Talking to a professional may provide you with the emotional support you seek in order move through your struggles and enhance your well-being.
