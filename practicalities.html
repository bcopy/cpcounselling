Practicalities

Location

Counselling sessions take place in a tranquil room at the Ecumenical Centre in Geneva:

Room 493, Salève Wing
Ecumenical Centre
150 Route de Ferney
1218 Grand-Saconnex

The Ecumenical Centre is accessible by TPG bus lines 5, 28, and F (stop Crêts-de-Morillon).

Duration

Counselling sessions last 60 minutes.
To cancel an appointment, please give at least 24 hours’ notice in order to avoid being charged.

Fee

100 CHF per 60-minute session.

Please inquire about sliding scale rates if you have financial restrictions.
Payment may be made in cash or electronic funds transfer at the end of each month.
Sessions are not reimbursable by health insurance schemes.
